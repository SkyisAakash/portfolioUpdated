Suffragist, celebrating 100th anniversary of 19th Amendment

Suffragist page on Ancestry.com was launched as part of #makeThemCount campaign to celebrate 100th anniversary of 19th amendment. The page allows users to look for their ancestors that might have fought in the women's suffrage movement. The page was talked about on television show the view on abc and was also features in multiple online blogs.

It was a great pleasure to be a key part of the team working on this project and contribute in helping countless people around the globe to understand the significance of women's suffrage movement.
